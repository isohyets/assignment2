Courses

Choosing the RIGHT COURSES

In Martial House, our training focuses on routines. These routines are classified into two broad categories, traditional and contemporary.

We offer distinct courses, specially designed to cater to different training purposes of our students!

Beginner

This course is for those who are interested in the introductory classes to Wushu, as well as for those who are keen in building a strong foundation.

Intermediate

This course is for students who have built a foundation, and are looking to expand their knowledge through practise of routines.

Competitive/Elite

This course is for athletes focused on perfecting their skills, aiming for the highest level of proficiency.
